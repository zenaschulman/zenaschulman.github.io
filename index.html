Product Manager @ Evol8tion

At Evol8tion, I manage the development and maintenance of Pipeline—a dynamic database of over 6,000 startups that we use internally and white label for our brand clients. As product manager, I spend most of my time defining product requirements, mapping user experiences, conducting market and user research, and communicating our Pipeline vision to various stakeholders.

In addition to owning our product, I manage content creation and strategy, assist with brand storytelling, and support client services.

Activist and Grassroots Organizer

The Muslim-Jewish Solidarity Committee is a grassroots community where Jews, Muslims, and all are welcome. We build relationships to stand against hate through shared values and social action.

Since the MJSC’s founding in 2014, I’ve dedicated myself to our mission by organizing volunteer meetings, designing our logos and promotional materials, building strategic partnerships with community organizations, and planning events.

Dancer and community manager

Soon after moving to NYC in 2013, I discovered Liberated Movement’s open-level, donation-based dance classes, and was hooked. The studio quickly became my refuge.

After attending classes for years, I joined the LM team as community manager. I welcome students before class, manage our social media accounts and inquiries, design promotional materials, and use my nonprofit skills to help with fundraising.
