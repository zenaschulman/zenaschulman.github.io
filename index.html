Product Manager @ Fluent, LLC

Fluent, LLC, is a rapidly growing agile audience engine, fueled by first party consumer data. As Associate Product Manager, I am the product owner of two of our nascent products, and contribute to the larger network strategy. I spend most of my time analyzing user data to uncover untapped opportunities, and brainstorming creative solutions for audience and client optimization.

In addition to product management, I serve on the Diversity Taskforce, leveraging my vast knowledge of inclusion programs and best practices to strengthen internal culture and broaden recruitment initiatives.

Interfaith Community Organizer

The Muslim-Jewish Solidarity Committee is a grassroots community where Jews, Muslims, and all are welcome. We build relationships to stand against hate through shared values and social action.

Since the MJSC’s founding in 2014, I’ve dedicated myself to our mission by organizing volunteer meetings, designing our logos and promotional materials, building strategic partnerships with community organizations, and planning events.

City Director for Lesbians Who Tech

Lesbians Who Tech is a community of queer women and nonbinary folks in or around tech (and the people who love us). Since its launch in late 2012, LWT has become a community of over 12,000 queer women in 25 cities, including 5 international cities.

As a NYC Director, I work with the LWT staff and other local leaders to plan and execute events in collaboration with our sponsors, such as Yext, Spotify, and Twitter. I also act as a brand ambassador and community manager to maintain and grow our local chapter of LWT.
