User Personas

I developed two user personas, focusing on the following areas:
Who: Demographic description - age, background, occupation, marital/relationship status, living situation, interests and hobbies, etc.
What: End goals - what the user tries to plan.
Why: Motivations for making these plans.
How: Behaviors and processes the user currently employs to make plans and meet up with other people.
Challenges and pain points: Types of obstacles the user faces when making and following through with plans.
